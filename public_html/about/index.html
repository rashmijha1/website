about me

I'm pursuing a double major in mathematics (applied track) and computer science at Vanderbilt University, with an additional minor in South Asian language and culture. I was born in Bihar, India and I currently live in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

My passions lie in the potential uses of technology to promote human welfare and education, while combating issues like climate change and racial injustice. My relevant technical skills include C++, Java, Python, AI/ML, and web design.

I'm a media/publications lead for the YX Foundation, a hybrid coalition-design lab dedicated to creating and analyzing deep tech through the framework of critical race theory.

On campus, I serve as the chair of Vanderbilt Student Government's Campus Alliance committee, a volunteer with the Vanderbilt Climate Change Initiative, and a board member for my dance team, Vandy Raas :)

In my free time, you can find me reading, watching anime, or grinding out matches on chess.com.
